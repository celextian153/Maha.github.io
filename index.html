ABOUT US

Gacud Paradisers, Seizure Salad and Maharlika. My Guild Elders used to tell me stories about the old days, a time of peace when Bosspaps kept the balance between Maharlika, Gacud Paradisers, and Seizure Salad. But that all changed when the Gacud Paradisers attacked. Only Bosspaps mastered all the four elements. Only he could stop the ruthless Gacud Paradisers. But when the world needed him most, he vanished (with 350M). Years have passed and the Gacud Paradisers is nearing victory in the War. Two years ago, my members and the elders of my guild journeyed to the Black Zone to help fight against the Gacud Paradisers, leaving me and my brother to look after our Guild. Some people believe that Bosspaps was never reborn into the Maharlika, and that the cycle is broken. But I haven't lost hope. I still believe that somehow, Bosspaps will return to save the world.
